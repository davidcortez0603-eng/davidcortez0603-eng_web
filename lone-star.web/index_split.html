FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Here are answers to some of the common questions homeowners ask when exploring a private sale.

Do I have to make repairs before selling?
No. We purchase properties as-is, even if they need major repairs or updates.
Are there fees, commissions, or closing costs?
No. We don’t charge fees or commissions. In most cases, we cover standard closing costs.
How quickly can you make an offer?
Many properties can receive an offer within 24–48 hours after we review the details.
Do you list properties on the MLS?
No. We are not acting as real estate agents or brokers. We purchase properties directly and privately.
What if I’m behind on payments or facing foreclosure?
We can review your situation quickly and privately so you can understand your options. There’s never any pressure to move forward.
What if the home has tenants?
We review tenant-occupied properties as well. Whether the tenants are month-to-month or under a lease, we can discuss options.
What areas do you buy in?
We consider properties across Texas, including major metros and surrounding areas.
What if I'm not sure I want to sell yet?
That’s completely fine. Requesting an offer is simply a way to explore options. You’re never obligated to say yes.
